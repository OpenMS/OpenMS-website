      <h2 class="openms-lib-block__title">From pipeline to browser</h2>
      <p class="openms-lib-block__lead">
        OpenMS WebApps provides an opinionated template for wrapping pyOpenMS and TOPP workflows
        in a user-friendly interface. Scientists can test workflows online or download packaged
        builds for local deployment.
