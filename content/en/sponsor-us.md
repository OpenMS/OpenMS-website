  <article class="governance-card">
    <p class="governance-card__eyebrow">Impact</p>
    <h2>Why sponsor</h2>
    <p>
      OpenMS powers proteomics and metabolomics workflows worldwide. Sponsorship helps
      the project maintain core libraries, web applications, and training resources
      that researchers rely on every day.
    </p>
    <ul>
      <li>Funded development and maintenance of OpenMS-lib and pyOpenMS</li>
      <li>Documentation, tutorials, and community support</li>
      <li>Developer meetings, workshops, and outreach</li>
    </ul>
  </article>
  <article class="governance-card">
    <p class="governance-card__eyebrow">OpenMS Inc.</p>
    <h2>How sponsorship works</h2>
    <p>
      OpenMS Inc. is a U.S. 501(c)(3) public charity that stewards project finances
      and accepts sponsorships aligned with our nonprofit mission.
    </p>
    <ul>
      <li>Organizational sponsorships and grants</li>
      <li>In-kind support for infrastructure or services</li>
      <li>Co-branded community initiatives where appropriate</li>
    </ul>
    <p class="governance-card__link">
      <a href="/about/">About OpenMS Inc. →</a>
    </p>
  </article>
</section>
