The OpenMS team and Steffen Neumann from CIBI will teach four workshops in South Africa:
### 9 and 10 February 2026, University of Western Cape, Cape Town: Plant Proteomics and Metabolomics
This course highlights the technologies of metabolomics and proteomics for plant biotechnology.  Equal time will be given to lectures and hands-on exercises. Topics include an introduction to biological liquid chromatography tandem mass spectrometry (LC-MS/MS), targeted and untargeted methods for metabolomics, metabolite identification, quantitation in LC-MS/MS, web resources for plan molecular biology, ontologies for plan research, and RNA-Seq for organisms lacking published genomes.
